FAQ

What happens after I report?

Your report is reviewed by our cybercrime experts within 24-48 hours. If verified, appropriate action is taken including content removal and legal proceedings if necessary.

Is my information safe?

Yes, all reports are handled with strict confidentiality. Anonymous reports require no personal information.

Can I track my report status?

Use your reference ID to check report status on our tracking page or call our helpline.
